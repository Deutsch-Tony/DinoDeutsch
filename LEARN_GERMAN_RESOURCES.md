# Learn German Resources

Ngay cap nhat: 2026-03-22

Ghi chu:
- "Toan bo" theo nghia tuyet doi la khong kha thi vi so luong trang hoc tieng Duc rat lon va thay doi lien tuc.
- Tai lieu nay la bo tong hop rong, uu tien cac nguon uy tin, dang hoat dong, va co ich de hoc thuc te.

## 1. Nen tang chinh thong / chat luong cao

### Goethe-Institut
- Link: https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/kur/doln.html
- Dung de: hoc bai ban, hoc theo CEFR A1-C2, luyen thi Goethe-Zertifikat.
- Diem manh:
  - Thuong duoc xem la nguon chuan va uy tin nhat cho tieng Duc hoc thuat.
  - Co khoa hoc online co giao vien, tu hoc, luyen noi, luyen thi.
  - Co lo trinh ro rang theo cap do.

### Goethe Deutschstunde
- Link: https://www.goethe.de/prj/dlp/en
- Dung de: tim tai lieu day va hoc, bai tap, tai nguyen phan theo trinh do A1-C2.
- Diem manh:
  - Ranh mach theo level.
  - Phu hop cho ca nguoi tu hoc va xay noi dung giao duc.

### vhs-Lernportal
- Link tong quan: https://a1.vhs-lernportal.de/wws/553396.php
- Link nen tang: https://www.vhs-lernportal.de/wws/kursleitende.php
- Dung de: hoc mien phi tu A1-C1, bao gom nghe, doc, viet, noi.
- Diem manh:
  - Nen tang mien phi cua Deutscher Volkshochschul-Verband.
  - Co khoa hoc A1-C1, ABC course, va ca khoa hoc phuc vu cong viec.
  - Phu hop nguoi hoc dai han, can lo trinh on dinh.

### Deutsche Welle - Nicos Weg
- Link video tong hop A1: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/4-eDoThe6qo
- Dung de: hoc qua video, hoi thoai, boi canh doi song thuc te.
- Diem manh:
  - Rat noi tieng voi nguoi moi hoc.
  - Hoc qua tinh huong, de nho hon hoc bang danh sach tu don le.
- Ghi chu:
  - Ket qua tim kiem web cho thay Nicos Weg van duoc DW duy tri; mot so trang DW bi robots chan nen minh khong mo truc tiep duoc tat ca trang con.

## 2. Nguon ngu phap va bai tap

### Schubert-Verlag Online-Aufgaben
- Link: https://www.schubert-verlag.de/aufgaben/
- Dung de: bai tap ngu phap, nghe, doc, viet online.
- Diem manh:
  - Nhieu bai tap co dap an.
  - Co ban PDF trong nhieu muc.
  - Rat hop de luyen bo sung hang ngay.

### Lingolia German
- Test trinh do: https://deutsch.lingolia.com/en/test
- Plus exercises: https://www.lingolia.com/en/plus
- Sitemap bai tap: https://www.lingolia.com/en/plus/sitemap
- Dung de: hoc va luyen ngu phap theo level A1-C1.
- Diem manh:
  - Co test nhanh xac dinh trinh do.
  - Co he thong bai tap lon, sap theo cap do va chu de ngu phap.
  - Tot cho viec luyen deuklination, cases, tenses, sentence structure.

### DeutschAkademie
- Link: https://www.deutschakademie.de/online-deutschkurs/english/german-grammar/
- Dung de: bai tap ngu phap online.
- Diem manh:
  - Pho bien trong cong dong hoc tieng Duc.
  - Thuong duoc dung de khoan luyen mot diem ngu phap cu the.
- Ghi chu:
  - Domain nay bi robots chan trong cong cu tim kiem o mot so truy van, nhung van la mot nguon pho bien de tham khao.

## 3. Tu dien va tra cuu

### Duden
- Link: https://www.duden.de/woerterbuch
- Dung de: tra chinh ta, nghia, ngu phap, cach dung, phat am, nguon goc tu.
- Diem manh:
  - Rat manh cho nguoi hoc muon viet dung, hieu dung, hoc dung van pham.
  - Co thong tin ve danh tu, dong tu, bien cach, dong nghia.

### LEO
- Link: https://www.leo.org/itde/index_en.html
- Dung de: tra tu, vi du, forum hoi dap, vocab trainer.
- Diem manh:
  - Tu dien rat manh cho cap ngon ngu Anh-Duc va nhieu ngon ngu khac voi tieng Duc.
  - Co forum huu ich khi gap sac thai nghia va cach dung kho.

### dict.cc
- Link mobile search: https://denl.pocket.dict.cc/
- Link thong tin he thong: https://eneo.dict.cc/
- Dung de: tra nhanh, audio, inflection, nhieu cap ngon ngu.
- Diem manh:
  - Co quy mo du lieu lon.
  - Tim nhanh, hop de tra tu hang ngay.

### Wiktionary
- Link: https://en.wiktionary.org/
- Dung de: tra etymology, chia dong tu, bien cach, tu dong nghia.
- Diem manh:
  - Tot khi muon hieu goc tu va quan he giua cac tu.
- Luu y:
  - Noi dung cong dong, can doi chieu voi Duden/LEO khi can do chinh xac cao.

## 4. Phat am va nghe

### Forvo
- Link ngon ngu Duc: https://forvo.com/pronounce/de/
- Link thong ke ngon ngu: https://forvo.com/languages/
- Dung de: nghe nguoi that phat am tu va cum tu.
- Diem manh:
  - Du lieu phat am tieng Duc rat lon.
  - Tot de kiem tra trong am va cach doc tu rieng le.

### Easy German Podcast
- Link: https://www.easygerman.org/podcast
- Dung de: luyen nghe thuc te, hoc cum tu, hoc van hoa.
- Diem manh:
  - 2 so moi moi tuan theo mo ta trang.
  - Co transcript tuong tac va vocabulary helper cho thanh vien.
  - Rat hop tu A2 tro len.

### Easy German
- Link chinh: https://www.easygerman.org/
- Dung de: video street interview, ngon ngu doi thuong, van hoa Duc.
- Diem manh:
  - Giup nghe giong that va cach noi that.
  - Rat tot de vuot khoi kieu hoc "sach giao khoa".

## 5. Tu vung va flashcard

- Link: https://ankiweb.net/
- Dung de: hoc flashcard theo SRS.
- Diem manh:

  - Tot cho noun genders, plural, verb forms, collocations.
- Ghi chu:
  - Chat luong deck rat khac nhau. Nen loc ky.

### Vi du deck cong dong moi

- Luu y:
  - Deck cong dong co the bien mat, doi link, hoac khong dong deu ve chat luong.

## 9. Goi y cach bien bo tai nguyen nay thanh website

Neu ban muon bien thanh website hoc tieng Duc, co the chia du lieu thanh cac nhom:
- `courses`: Goethe, VHS, DW
- `grammar`: Lingolia, Schubert, DeutschAkademie
- `dictionary`: Duden, LEO, dict.cc, Wiktionary
- `pronunciation`: Forvo
- `listening`: Easy German, podcasts, DW videos
- `flashcards`: Anki, tu tao deck
- `level-tests`: Lingolia test, LanguageLevel

Moi item nen co:
- `name`
- `url`
- `category`
- `price` (`free`, `freemium`, `paid`)
- `levels` (`A1`, `A2`, ...)
- `skills` (`reading`, `listening`, `grammar`, ...)
- `notes`
- `official` (`true/false`)
